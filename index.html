Where is Lethal Company Outperforming Call of Duty?

Lethal Company is an indie game produced by one man, and it has been outperforming Call of Duty, but by how much, and where?

by Eliot Force | Dec. 15, 2023

The map below compares the performance of the game Lethal Company with the performance of the new Call of Duty internationally.

The results show that Lethal Company is far more popular than Call of Duty in English speaking countries. This demonstrates just how much the game’s sales have been fueled by online content, as internet users outside of english-speaking circles do not seem to have caught on to the game as much. China, with its separate internet ecosystem, had a statistically insignificant amount of Lethal Company sales despite their large gaming market.

Award-winning game designer Nicholas Fortugno says Lethal Company’s intelligent game design creates incentives to work as a team, but also to betray each other. This makes for interesting and entertaining moments which people like to share online, and viral clips of the game have fueled its popularity.

“Players are constantly pressed to make choices to work together or abandon each other, and the risk/reward balance of that is great,” Fortugno said. “When players tell stories about the game, that's what they talk about.”

Lethal Company’s unassuming graphics and low budget would cause it to fly under most people’s radar if it didn’t have a viral craze associated with it. In places where the it hasn’t been bought much, it is likely that the game hasn't been trending on social media.
